How The Pet Wheel Works

The Pet Wheel Spinner is a fun and fair way to choose between your pets for activities like:

  • Feeding time: Can't decide which pet to feed first? Let the wheel decide!
  • Playtime: If you have limited time to play with your pets, use the wheel to select fairly.
  • Grooming sessions: Let the wheel pick which pet gets groomed first.
  • Vet visits: When scheduling multiple appointments, use the wheel to decide the order.

Using the Pet Wheel is simple:

  1. Enter your pet's name in the input field
  2. Click "Add" to add them to the wheel (add as many as you like)
  3. Click "Spin!" to randomly select a pet
  4. The wheel will spin and select a winner!

The Pet Wheel uses a fair random selection algorithm to ensure every pet has an equal chance of being chosen.

Pet Care Tips

Proper pet care is essential for your furry friends' health and happiness. Here are some helpful tips:

Feeding Schedule

Establish a consistent feeding schedule for your pets. Most adult dogs should be fed twice daily, while cats may prefer multiple small meals. Always provide fresh water and monitor portion sizes to prevent obesity.

Regular Exercise

All pets need regular exercise to maintain physical and mental health. Dogs need daily walks, while cats benefit from interactive play sessions with toys that stimulate their hunting instincts.

Grooming Routine

Regular grooming helps keep your pet's coat healthy and reduces shedding. Brush your dog or cat several times a week, trim nails monthly, and schedule professional grooming as needed for longer-haired breeds.

Veterinary Care

Schedule regular check-ups with your veterinarian, keep vaccinations up to date, and stay current on flea, tick, and heartworm prevention. Early detection of health issues can save your pet's life.
